RATAN TATA

Chairman Emeritus
  • Timeline
  • Achievements

  • Education
  • 1937
    Born in Bombay, British India.
  • 1955
    Enrolled at Cornell University for Architecture and Structural Engineering.
  • 1962
    Graduated from Cornell University in Architecture.
  • 1971
    Appointed Director-in-Charge of National Radio & Electronics Company Limited (NELCO).
  • 1981
    Named Chairman of Tata Industries.
  • 1991
    Became Chairman of Tata Sons, succeeding J.R.D. Tata.
  • 1998
    Launched Tata Indica, India's first indigenously developed car.
  • 2008
    Led Tata Motors to acquire Jaguar & Land Rover.
  • 2012
    Retired as Chairman of Tata Sons, later returned briefly in 2016.
  • 2022
    Opened a cancer hospital in Assam.

Early Life

Ratan Tata was born on December 28, 1937, in Bombay (now Mumbai), India. He was raised by his grandmother, Navajbai Tata, after his parents separated when he was just ten years old. Growing up in a prestigious business family, he was exposed to leadership and responsibility from an early age.

Educational Background

  • Schooling: Campion School, Mumbai & Cathedral and John Connon School, Mumbai
  • Pre-University: Bishop Cotton School, Shimla & Riverdale Country School, New York
  • Bachelor's Degree: Cornell University (1962) - Architecture and Structural Engineering
  • Advanced Management Program: Harvard Business School (1975)
  • Honorary Doctorates: Multiple honorary degrees from universities including the University of Warwick and Ohio State University
  • Fellowships: Honorary Fellow of the Royal Academy of Engineering
  • Other Courses: Various executive courses in business management and leadership
